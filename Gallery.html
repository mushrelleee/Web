Photo Album

moments captured on camera

Taylor Swift has had a career full of unforgettable highlights — here are some of the best caught on camera.

(April 2009) The Fearless Tour: Taylor's first ever headlining tour, a 52 cities tour that generated over 62 million in gross revenue.

(August 2009) At the MTV Video Music Awards, Kanye West jumped onstage during Swift’s acceptance speech for Best Female Video, grabbed her microphone and told the crowd that Beyonce’s “Single Ladies” clip was more worthy of the award.

(November 2009) At 19 years old, Swift became the youngest Entertainer of the Year winner at the Country Music Association Awards when she took home the top prize, making once again history.

(January 2010) Best Female Country Vocal Performance for “White Horse” was the first Grammy Swift ever won. This would be the start of a chain of Grammys that Taylor Swift later brought home. At the age of 20, Swift became (and remains to this day) the youngest artist to win the prestigious Album of the Year award.

(December 2011) Taylor Swift acquires the title of "Billboard's Woman of the Year", capping off a year of success.

(March 2023) Taylor Swift performing in Glendale, Arizona, at the first show of The Eras Tour. It's a start of a new era.

(November 2022) Taylor Swift, after bringing home yet again AMAs, becomes the artist with the most AMAs awarded, topping with 32 wins.
