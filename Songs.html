Taylor Swift tells stories through her songs, shaping the lyrics into something beautiful that otherwise would've remained untold. Several myths revolve around her songs, such as the 5th song of each ablum being her most vulnerable one, or that each song is dedicated to someone, like her exes. In this section of the website, we'll be diving into the deeper meaning of her songs and breaking them down as well as analyzing her lyrics, but first, get to know us.

About us

"Swifties" is the official name of her fandom. With over 150 million of us around the world, Swifties are passionate connoisseurs of Taylor's music. We make up a powerful fanbase and are desperate for her concert tickets!! Although we might share different opinions on what her best songs are, we most oftenly agree on which are the classics and most iconic. Being one myself, I've created this website in support of Taylor Swift. There is no excat distinction in what makes someone a Swiftie, you can join us without owning albums, and you're obviously more than welcomed if your obsession with her surpasses everything else!
